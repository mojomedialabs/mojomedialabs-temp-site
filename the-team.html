Meet Our Team

Michael Rose

CEO

Michael Rose, speaker, author and entrepreneur is the Founder and CEO of Return on Energy, Inc, as well as Founder and CEO of Rose Group Companies; MOJO Media Labs, a strategic and creative marketing agency and Marketing Candy™ a print and promotional product company.

Over fourteen years ago, Michael set out on a mission to deliver quality branded products to meet his customers’ needs. As an innovator set on solving problems and delivering value, the business evolved over the years to meet the changing needs of their loyal clients. MOJO Media Labs evolved from Michael’s love and strong knowledge of small to medium sized businesses, coupled with the strategic marketing know-how to deliver quantifiable marketing results that impact what matters most to MOJO’s clients: New client acquisition and client retention & growth.

Michael’s strong knowledge and understanding of business, as well as marketing best practices has evolved to him authoring and speaking on a new and innovative game-changing approach to business entitled ROE Powers ROI. His methodology is the secret sauce behind MOJO’s success and that of our customers.

Keri Heye

Managing Director

Keri provides strategic planning and program management support to companies across a broad range of industries, and is a key member of the agency’s day-to-day leadership team. Prior to joining MOJO, Keri managed Field Marketing programs for a national environmental services company, and provided key sales and marketing support to business development teams as part of the corporate marketing team for a global technology services provider. In addition to her client-side experience, Keri spent almost 10 years as a consultant; helping clients formulate and implement growth strategies, executing business development campaigns, and facilitating post-merger integration programs. She holds undergraduate and graduate business degrees from Clemson University and Southern Methodist University.

Tracy Southers

PR Expert

Tracy Southers, APR, is a senior level PR specialist with more than 18 years professional experience. Her career includes corporate positions in telecommunications, real estate, hospitality, and technology; as well as serving as the public information officer for the City of Southlake, Texas. Southers attended The University of Texas, Austin, and received a bachelor of arts degree in journalism from The University of Texas, Arlington. She attained the national Accreditation in Public Relations (APR) designation in 2003. Southers is a member of the Public Relations Society of America, past president of the PRSA Gulf Coast Chapter, a Gulfshore Business magazine’s Top 40 Under 40 business professional for 2007, and contributing writer to N.E.T Business Resource magazine. Southers hails from Fort Worth, Texas, and moved to Naples in 2000. She returned to the Dallas-Fort Worth area in 2008 and currently resides in Grapevine, Texas.
